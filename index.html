Introduction

    Sarvesh college draws on a reserve of Sarvesh college draws on a reserve of extraordinary talent as a by-product of its selective, stringent and consequently, amazingly successful to all the UG programmes. The quality of students coming in is, without doubt, the best in the world- drilled in the sciences and problem-solving, the successful candidates are, by default, gifted, dedicated, hard working and blessed with analytical ability. The pool of talent thus uncovered embraces the best and the brightest from across the length and breadth of the nation. People from the most diverse backgrounds come together in their quest to extend the frontiers of scientific and technological knowledge and in the process forge life-long friendships and inculcate the spirit of teamwork and dedication, which gives them a head start in terms of their careers in the distant corners of the globe. The institute offers a wide range of subjects for the various in demand courses under the proper mentorship and guidance of our highly qualified professors and faculties. The institute believes strongly in their students and thus work hand in hand for the overall development and growth of their students that would indeed play an important role in attracting the young brains of the nation and thus serving them with the quality UG level education.

From Principal Desk

It is indeed a great pleasure to serve this institution, Sarvesh College. We believe that students from nearby towns join the institute for their overall development in terms of academics, extra curricular activities, leadership skills and much more. We have a firm motto to serve these students so that they stand sound amidst this competitive environment. We have various competitive courses being offered at our institute under the supervision of highly qualified faculty members. We do conduct regular interactive activities to boost the confidence of the students, and for a perfect engagement for them too. We are planning to innovate the institute and be technology driven to cope with the industry standards and be in tune with that. We do have all the basic amenities and facilities at our institute with a disciplined environment for an overall growth of the institute as well.

At this fast evolving institute, our focus is on providing an ambience conducive to developing a career rather than to just getting a job. I am delighted to inform you that, the college has earned its place amongst the upcoming institutes of excellence, with a potential to develop into a world-class centre of higher technical education over the coming years. I wish all my students, a great success in all their endeavours

Sarvesh College is situated at 3STR Bikaner Road, New Gharsana in the District of Shri GangaNagar of Rajasthan. The assembly constitute for the institute is located at Anupgarh and the parliamentary constituency is Bikaner and both of these are reserved.
